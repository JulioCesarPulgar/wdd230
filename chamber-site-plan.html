Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

The Springfield Chamber of Commerce

Reason: Springfield is a thriving town with a diverse business community, and the chamber aims to promote and support local businesses.

Site Purpose

  • To promote local businesses and economic development.
  • To attract tourists and visitors to the area.
  • To provide resources and support for chamber members.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What are the current demographics and events in the Springfield area?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #004d99 (used for headings, accents)
  • Secondary Color: #ff9900 (used for buttons, highlights)

Typography

  • Main Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text)
  • Heading Font: Georgia, serif (used for headings)

Wireframe

Developmental Specifications

  • The site will be developed using valid HTML, CSS, and JavaScript technology.
  • The design will be consistent throughout the site, providing ease of navigation and supporting positive user experiences.
  • The site will support responsive design techniques to ensure it is accessible on all devices.
  • The home page will contain attention-grabbing information, community updates, weather, calls to action, and business spotlights.
  • The discover page will include local history, demographics, current events, and an image montage of interesting area sites.
  • The directory page will list local businesses and organizations that belong to the chamber of commerce.
  • The join page will contain information about membership benefits, including three membership levels (non-profit, silver, and gold) with different perks.
